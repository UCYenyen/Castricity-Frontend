Capabilities

Built for Operators. Trusted by Decision-Makers.

🔮

Hybrid AI Engine

Prophet captures temporal seasonality; LightGBM corrects residuals using weather, lag, and calendar features.

🔍

SHAP Explainability

Every prediction comes with Top-3 contributing factors, quantified in MWh, with direction indicators.

🛡️

Anomaly Detection

Isolation Forest flags abnormal input data before prediction, preventing decisions based on corrupt readings.

🗺️

What-If Simulator

Simulate impact of temperature extremes, rainfall, and national holidays on electricity demand in real-time.

🔌

100% Offline

All models run locally (.joblib). No OpenAI, AWS, or GCP. Full digital sovereignty for critical infrastructure.

⚡

Zero Data Leakage

Chronological 70/15/15 split, KNNImputer fit-only-on-train, per-split feature engineering — guaranteed integrity.
